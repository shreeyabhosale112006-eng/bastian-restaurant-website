About Us

Welcome to Bastian, where culinary artistry meets heartfelt hospitality. Nestled in the heart of Pune, Bastian is more than just a dining destination—it's an experience that tantalizes your taste buds and warms your soul.

At Bastian, we believe in the magic of food and the stories it tells. Our menu is a harmonious blend of traditional flavors and contemporary techniques, crafted with the finest ingredients and a passion for perfection. Each dish is a testament to our commitment to quality, creativity, and sustainability.

Our ambiance reflects the essence of Bastian: elegant yet cozy, sophisticated yet unpretentious. Whether you’re here for a romantic dinner, a family celebration, or a casual meal with friends, we strive to create memorable moments that linger long after the last bite.

Our dedicated team, from our talented chefs to our attentive servers, is here to ensure that every visit to Bastian is a delightful journey. We take pride in our warm hospitality, personalized service, and the genuine connections we build with our guests.

Join us at Bastian, where every meal is a celebration, and every guest is family. Discover the flavors, the stories, and the joy of dining with us. Welcome to your new favorite place.
